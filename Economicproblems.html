Economic Problems. What is affecting our economy? Here are some of the major problems affecting our economy today. Inflation Rate. What is Inflation Rate and how does it affect us? Inflation is the rate of increase in prices over a given period of time. Inflation in the Philippines as of March 2023 is 7.6% so it has decreased because the chart of inflation as of January 2023 was 8.7%. So how does it affect us? For example if we talk about the price of onions in the Philippines. The price of onions became overpriced with an amount of 250 pesos per kilograms and this has become an issue because of the price of onions.

Poverty. Poverty has been a very big problem to our economy with a rapid increase within it. Why is poverty a problem? Poverty demands the lack of income and productive resources to ensure sustainable livelihoods. How is it a problem in the economy? The higher rate of poverty, the lower the rate of unemployment. Many report are saying that poverty is positive in unemployment rate. Poverty is a state of an individual who lacks the financial means to cover basic living expenses. It also limits the access to educational and employment oppurtunities because the Philippines education system requires significant improvement to meet the demands of the modern economy. Many filipinos lack the skills needed for high paying jobs limiting their economic potential.

Agriculture. Agriculture is a crtical issue in the Philippines, but it faces several challenges such as low productivity, insufficient infrastructure and natural disasters. Our agricultural sector needs more investment to increase the country's productivity and improve food security.

Overall, these economic problems needs more investment and changes to address the situation effectively. The goverment, Private sectors and the community need to work together to address all the issue and improve the country's economy.
